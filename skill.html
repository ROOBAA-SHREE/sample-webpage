AN OVERVIEW


The Bannari Amman Institute of Technology was established in 1996 by the Bannari Amman Group, serves as one of the citadels of higher education in the Southern part of India. BIT aids to impart knowledge, teamwork, innovation, entrepreneurship, courage, sacrifice and duty which are innards of a meaningful life.A serene place for learning, discovery, innovation, expression and discourseBIT has transformed and ingrained various cutting edge administrative and academic reforms. The Institute caters to the needs of the students and members of faculty in realising thier dreams and aspirations. BIT has been conferred with a number of accolades and recognitions from top-notch forums of high eminence.
